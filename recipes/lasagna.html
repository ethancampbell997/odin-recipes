Lasagna

Lasagna is a classic dish that everyone loves! It has all the meat, cheese, and pasta that one could ever want!

This recipe is the top rated lasagna recipe on allrecipes.com, so you're in for a treat!

Ingredients

  • Meat: Sweet italian sausage and lean ground beef
  • 1 Onion
  • 2 Cloves of garlic
  • 1 Can of crushed tomatoes
  • 2 Cans of tomato sauce
  • 2 Cans of tomato paste
  • 2 Tablespoons of white sugar
  • Parsley
  • Basil leaves
  • Salt
  • Italian seasoning
  • Fennel seeds
  • Black pepper
  • Lasagna noodes
  • Parmesan
  • Mozzarella
  • Ricotta
  • Egg

Steps

  1. Make the meat sauce
  2. Cook the noodles
  3. Make the ricotta mixture
  4. Layer the lasagna (steps 5-11)
  5. Meat sauce
  6. Noodles
  7. Ricotta mixture
  8. Mozzarella slices
  9. Meat sauce
  10. Parmesan cheese
  11. Repeat the layers, then top with the remaining Parmesan
  12. Cover with foil and bake
  13. Let the lasagna rest before serving
